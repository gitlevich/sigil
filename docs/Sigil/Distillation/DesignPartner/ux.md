# DesignPartner

I am in conversation with the sigil. The partner responds from inside the trajectory I am currently in — it speaks the domain language of this sigil, knows the invariants I have declared, and can see what is contained here.

I can ask it anything about the current structure. It will respond in domain terms — not generic AI, but the voice of the accumulated narration. When I teach it something new, it writes that into the sigil directly. The conversation and the structure are the same thing.

When the partner detects an incoherence — a gap, a contradiction, a trajectory that is too thin to project — it surfaces it as a question or a proposed edit. I can accept or redirect.

I can switch between @AI providers in the conversation header. Multiple conversations per sigil, each named. The most recent opens by default.
