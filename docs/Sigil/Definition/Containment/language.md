# Containment

A sigil is recursive: it is made of sigils. This is not a parent-child relationship — it is containment. The contained sigils inhabit the context of the containing sigil. They share that context.

The limit of five comes from how human attention works: I cannot hold more than five to seven separate concepts at any level of detail. Complexity visible in any one context is always bounded.
