# Entanglement

When two agents of the same @scale interact, their @sigils intersect in contrast space: they are attending to a shared section of the space where they have overlapping @preferences over mutually-relevant @invariants. 

While entangled, the @agents mutually affect each other's narratives. 

I call it "entanglement" because it rhymes with @collapse and sounds like quantum mechanics. I am allowed: my @sigil, my rules.
