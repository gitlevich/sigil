# Scale

Neighbor @sigils are at the same scale. That lets them entangle with each other (@entanglement).

A @sigil cannot entangle with @sigils at other scales: neither with those it contains, nor with the @sigil containing it. 

Example: I can entangle with other people, dogs, birds, insects and elefants. I cannot entangle with my cells (@sigils I consist of) or my family (I am its part).
