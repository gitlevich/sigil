A shape in @contrastSpace, centered on its @fixedPoint. Closed over the @contrasts it cares about — within it, @preferences hold along those axes (!relevant-preferences-enforced). Open along all other @contrasts — their values are in superposition.

Powered by the @attention of the @agent that #inhabits it. Filter @observations by relevant @contrasts, @collapse into @narrative.

To an @agent, any @sigil is a pattern recognized and preferred along the distance axis.

Emergent @sigils are powered by multiple @agents' attentions: the market, a language, a constitution. @Agents agree to follow shared @invariants, creating structure at a higher @scale.

@EcologicalPsychology@surface describes the @sigil boundary.
