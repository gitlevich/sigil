# Light Cone

The boundary between @timelike and @spacelike directions at a point. Separates what can influence the point (past @lightCone) from what the point can influence (future @lightCone). Everything outside is causally disconnected.

The @observer can #see-the-boundary — from any @fixedPoint, at a given @resolution, what is reachable and what is not. Inside the cone: reachable trajectories. Outside: causally disconnected, no matter how much @attention is spent.

!causal-boundary: nothing crosses the @lightCone from outside to inside. The boundary between @timelike and @spacelike is absolute — it does not depend on the @observer's position or motion, only on the structure of the @manifold.

In @attention: the @lightCone at a @fixedPoint bounds what is reachable from it at a given @resolution. The inverse @lightCone of a @sigil's vocabulary is the set of all expressible trajectories — everything that can be said in the domain language. Sentences inside land; sentences outside fall off the edge.
