# Surface

The interface between substance and medium. @Surfaces are the primary structural unit of the @Environment — they have layout, texture, reflectance, and resistance. An @Organism does not perceive atoms or fields; it perceives @Surfaces. The arrangement of @Surfaces constitutes places, objects, openings, and barriers. @Surfaces structure the ambient energy arrays, creating the @Information that @Perception picks up. Most @Affordances are @Affordances of @Surfaces and their layout.
