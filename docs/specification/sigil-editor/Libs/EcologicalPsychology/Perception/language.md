# Perception

The direct pickup of @Information from ambient energy arrays. @Perception is not the processing of sensory inputs into internal representations. It is the detection of @Invariants in structured @Information — @Invariants that specify the @Environment and its @Affordances. @Perception is active: the @Organism orients, adjusts, and explores to make @Information available. @Perception and action are continuous — the @Organism perceives in order to act and acts in order to perceive.
