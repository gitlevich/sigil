# Measure Affordance Affinity

For each affordance, ratio of distance-to-parent vs distance-to-nearest-uncle in @ContrastSpace. Below 1 = correctly placed. Above 1 = closer to an uncle, probably misplaced. Aggregate: fraction correctly placed.

Confirms sibling separation from the affordance's perspective. An affordance equidistant to two siblings belongs on neither — it belongs on their parent.
