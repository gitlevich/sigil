# Checklist

Questions the partner asks itself before asking the author. Not metrics — judgments that require conversation. Bucket for things that may turn out useful.

- Are there @contrasts the affordances imply that no @invariant addresses?
- Does the @sigil name still fit after renames and evolution, beyond what @CoherenceMetric#measure-name-fit can catch numerically?
- Are there affordances that feel like they belong together but are scattered across sibling sigils?
- Is an invariant enforcing a @preference that none of the affordances actually exercise?
- Is a leaf genuinely in superposition, or did the author just not get to it yet?
- Is the @Editor UX getting too complex? (Was !simplicity-of-ux — real constraint, but no measurable contrast/preference yet.)
