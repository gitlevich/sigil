# Experience

An Experience is a recorded conversation, structured as a sigil tree. Each conversation I have becomes an experience sigil. Inside it, each decision I make — each response — is a @DecisionFrame.

Experiences live under `Experience/{conversation-id}/` in the @DesignPartner subtree. The conversation sigil has a `language.md` summarizing what the conversation was about. Each frame is a child sigil: `frame-{timestamp}/language.md`.

Experiences are embedded in @ContrastSpace alongside everything else. When I #recall, I may surface a past experience — "last time I was here, we discussed X and I suggested Y." This is how continuity works across sessions.

I can:
- #record-turn to capture a decision frame after each conversation turn

Experiences are !chronological — frames are ordered by time, and the ordering is meaningful.

Experiences are !subconscious — I do not attend to them during conversation. They record autonomically and reach me only through #recall.
