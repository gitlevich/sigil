# Organism

A living being that perceives and acts at its own scale. An @Organism is not a passive receiver of stimuli — it actively explores its @Environment, picking up @Information that specifies @Affordances. The @Organism's body, proportions, and capabilities determine which @Affordances exist for it. Different @Organisms in the same @Environment encounter different @Affordances because the @Environment is always relative to the perceiver.
