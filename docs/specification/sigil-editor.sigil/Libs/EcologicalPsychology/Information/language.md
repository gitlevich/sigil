# Information

Structure in ambient energy arrays that specifies the @Environment. @Information is not a signal transmitted from world to mind. It is the pattern in light, sound, and other media that is structured by @Surfaces and their layout. The optic array, for example, carries @Information about @Surface shape, distance, texture, and @Affordance — all available for pickup by an @Organism through @Perception. @Information is inexhaustible: @Exploration reveals further structure at finer scales.
