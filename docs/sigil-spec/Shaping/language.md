# Shaping

in order to shape a sigil, I begin with narrating what it is that I want to happen there in the context of it. So I tell a story that describes what I am doing. I visualize myself in it, using its affordances to complete my goals within that sigil. This gets me thinking in the direction of the language that I'm gonna use. I'm gonna try a few different ways to say this. I'm gonna use different phrases because it's probable that I'm gonna do more than one thing in its context. I'm listening as I am narrating to the names of what, of the things, of the main concepts that perform these actions for me and to how I describe these actions. The names will eventually become the names of the contained sigils and the affordances will be distributed between them. In the end, I'll be able to express everything I need using the vocabulary I just described. The sigils and their affordances. And of course I am still limited to five because I don't want to get very complicated, super rich contexts where my attention will just get lost.
