# Shaping

I navigate to a sigil to shape it by speaking its affordances into existence. I express these affordances in terms of contained sigils that I speak into existence while defining affordances. The language creates the structure.

I inhabit the sigil. I close my eyes and imagine using its context. What do I reach for? What should be effortless? What constraints do I feel? As I write the narrative, the affordances and contained sigils emerge. I see how neighbors relate through the affordances they expose to each other. I reshape by renaming terms across the whole sigil, moving sigils between containers, splitting and merging.
